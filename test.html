Who we are

HypeBz started as a passion project by four Concordia University students who wanted a convenient and cheap way for local businesses to promote their services. HypeBz is focused on making an easy-to-use marketplace while also making social media and other promotion for partner companies. Our goal is for local companies to not have to deal with the hassles of administrating websites and marketing campaigns.
